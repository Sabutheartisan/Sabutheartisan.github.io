Veg Momos

  • Preparation Time: 1 hour
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 servings (12-14 momos approx)

Ingredients for Dough

Ingredients Quantity
All purpose flour (Maida) 3/4 cup
Cooking Oil 1 tablespoon
Salt As you like for taste

Ingredients for Stuffing

Ingredients Quantity
Finely chopped garlic 3-4 cloves
Cooking Oil 1 tablespoon
Finely chopped ginger 1 teaspoon
Finely choppedcarrot 1/2 cup
Finely chopped cabbage 1/2 cup
Finely chopped capsicum 1/4 cup
Finely chopped green beans 1/4 cup
Finely chopped green onion 1/4 cup
Soy sauce 1/2-1 teaspoon
Chilli sauce 1 teaspoon
Black pepper powder 1/2 teaspoon
Salt To taste cup

Directions:

Step 1: Finely cut all the vegetables as mentioned in the ingredients section.

Step 2: Add 3/4 cup of maida, 1 tablespoon oil and salt in a large bowl.

Step 3: Mix all ingredients well and prepare a soft pliable dough by adding water as needed. Cover the dough and keep it aside for 20-25 minutes.

Step 4: Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a pan over medium flame. Add finely chopped ginger and garlic and saute for 30 seconds. Add green onion and saute for a minute.

Step 5: Add all chopped veggies (cabbage, green beans, capsicum and carrot) and salt.

Step 6: Mix well and saute them for 4-5 minutes. Add 1 teaspoon chilli sauce.
Step 7: Add 1/2-1 teaspoon soy sauce.

Step 8: Add 1/2 tablespoon black pepper powder.

Step 9: Mix welland saute for a minute. Turn off the flame. Stuffing for veg momos is ready.

Step 10: Knead the dough again for a minute and divide it into two equal portions. Give each portion a round cylindrical shape like cucumber. Cut each one into 6-7 equal portions with a knife.

Step 11: Mix all ingredients well and prepare a soft pliable dough by adding water as needed. Cover the dough and keep it aside for 20-25 minutes.

Step 12: Give each small portion round shape like a ball and roll it with a rolling pin to make round flattened shape.

Step 13: Place approx 1 tablespoon filling in the center. Don't over-stuff it otherwise it will be difficult to get a proper "potli" shape.

Step 14: Lift the edge from one side and start pleating. Join them in the center to seal it.

Step 15: Place stand in the steamer and place momos plate over it. Cover it for 6-7 minutes over a medium flame. Remove the lid and check them by touching it. If they don't feel sticky then it means they are cooked.

Step 8: Transfer them to a serving plate. Serve hot Veg Momos with Momos Chutney or SchezwanSauce.
